About Ayoub

Ayoub - Founder & Head Tutor

Biography

I graduated from Imperial College London with a BSc degree in Physics with Theoretical Physics in 2020, and from University College London with an MSc degree in Medical Physics and Bioengineering in 2022. Since then, I have become a distinguished full-time tutor of Maths and Science for students aged 7 to 18.

Teaching Experience

With over 1,000 hours of online and in-person tutoring, I have helped students from various backgrounds and abilities fulfil their academic potential. My teaching experience includes, but is not limited to: homeschooling GCSE and A-Level students for periods of up to two years, working with local schools to run and teach 11+ preparation after-school clubs, and delivering continuous weekly after-school booster revision classes.

My specialist subjects are:

  • A-Level Maths and Physics
  • GCSE Maths, Physics, Chemistry and Biology
  • KS3 Maths and Science
  • 11+ Maths and Non-Verbal Reasoning
